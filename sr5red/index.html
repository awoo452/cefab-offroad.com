1985 SR5 4X4

This truck has just over 200k on the odometer. It is a factory 1985 SR5 longbed and is completely original from top to bottom. We're doing a full restoration / overhaul now, broken down by individual projects. Check them out below!

FULL DETAIL

After what appears to be several years of neglect, this truck's exterior needed some serious attention. With about 8 hours total between a full claybar treatment, polishing, buffing, and a coat of synthetic wax we were able to bring the OEM shine on this paint back to life!

WINDSHIELD REPLACEMENT

The ultimate goal here was to save the factory paint, so we opted to use a fiberglass filler and some expanded metal to patch the rust holes we found once the windshield was pulled. Huge shout-out to Jakes Glass LLC for pulling and re-installing the glass!
