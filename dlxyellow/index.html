1980 DLX 4X4

This Craigslist find is quite a survivor, and has proven to be a solid daily driver / work truck too! This one will stay OEM for the most part, with the addition of parts like the FJ40 wheels thanks to Cool Cruisers of Texas or the Gem Top canopy found on Facebook Marketplace!

2OR ENGINE OVERHAUL

A weeping headgasket out of the drivers side between cylinders 2 & 3 means it's time for some maintenance! New gaskets all around, fresh fluids, and more on the way for this rig.

WINDSHIELD REPLACEMENT

The ultimate goal here was to save the factory paint, so we opted to use a fiberglass filler and some expanded metal to patch the rust holes we found once the windshield was pulled. Huge shout-out to Jakes Glass LLC for pulling and re-installing the glass!

22R SWAP

That 20R was not long for this world, and looking back the entire headgasket / timing project proved to be some great experience, and not much else. This 22R swap came out of an 83 2WD pickup, and dropped right in after swapping over the 4WD oil pan and a few other parts.

KC LIGHTS

These KC lights came with the blue 1980 SR5, and ever since I picked up this DLX I always knew I wanted to put them on this one instead, but they've just kinda been sitting on the shelf since the mounting brackets on the push bar weren't in the right spot. Until now.
