16' Trailer Restoration

This restoration project included the removal of the old and badly damaged fenders, which were replaced with custom built fender frames and the use of reclaimed material for the fenders themselves. We also built a brace around the back half of the trailer to give it much more structural integrity, which also helps greatly reduce trailer flex when loading and unloading. The old lights and bracketry were removed and replaced with a set of used LED lights we had laying around, and finally a new deck was installed.
