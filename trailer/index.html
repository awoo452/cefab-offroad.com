16' TRAILER RESTORATION

We started with a 16' RV trailer that had been poorly converted to a flatbed trailer. We were able to salvage some of the material to rebuild the fenders after bracing the frame, but threw away most of the other junk we pulled off of this rolling road hazard. Once we rewired this trailer and installed some less stupid looking lights, a new 2x8 doug fir deck was secured using Teks Wood-to-Metal Screws, we have ourselves a trailer!
